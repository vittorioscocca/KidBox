Support

Have a problem, a question or a suggestion? KidBox support is built directly into the app — no emails, no external forms.

💬

AI-powered support chat

Open a direct chat with KidBox's AI assistant straight from Settings. Describe the problem in your own words, attach up to 5 screenshots to show what's wrong, and the assistant understands what you need.

How it works

✍️
Describe it in your own words No form to fill in. Simply write what is happening — the AI understands the context and already knows how to help you.
🖼️
Attach up to 5 screenshots Show exactly what's wrong. Screenshots help the assistant identify the problem without lengthy explanations.
🔍
Automatic bug detection If the assistant detects a bug, it asks your consent to automatically attach system logs — so the team already has everything needed to investigate.
📨
Send as a ticket with one tap At the end of the chat you can send the conversation as a support ticket. It reaches the team directly without any intermediate steps.
✅

System logs are attached only with your explicit consent and never contain personal data or your family's content.

How to open the support chat

1
Open KidBoxOn your iPhone or iPad
2
Go to SettingsTap the ⚙️ icon at the bottom right
3
Tap "Support"The AI assistant chat opens
4
Describe the problem and sendThe team receives the ticket and responds as soon as possible
ℹ️

The support chat is available to all KidBox users, including users on the free plan.
