The AI agents

Five specialists.
One single app.

Each agent knows your family, updates in real time and takes concrete action — it doesn't just answer questions.

🧠 Agent 1

L'Assistente di Famiglia

Talk to it like a personal assistant who knows every detail about your family. It knows what's on the calendar, the children's medications, the month's expenses and the tickets in the wallet. And it doesn't just answer: when you ask it to create an event or add a reminder, it does so directly.

Calendar Todo Expenses Shopping list Automatic actions
🩺 Agent 2

Il Consulente di Salute

For every family member, a medical assistant who knows appointments, tests, ongoing treatments, vaccines and uploaded reports. Ask questions in plain language and receive clear answers, designed for a parent — not a doctor.

Appointments Tests Vaccines PDF reports Treatments
✈️ Agent 3

Il Pianificatore di Viaggi

Tell it where you want to go, with whom and for how many days. The agent already knows your children's ages and any health conditions. It generates a complete itinerary, day by day, tailored to your family.

Personalized itineraries Family-friendly Regenerate days
⚡ Agent 4

La Mente Proattiva

Works for you even when the app is closed. Every morning a briefing with appointments, medications, scheduled vaccines, vehicle deadlines, home payments and children's birthdays. Every week a recap. Every month a children's health analysis with patterns and signals to watch.

Daily briefing Weekly recap Monthly health analysis Vehicle deadlines Birthdays
🔍 Agent 5

Document Intelligence

Import a document — an invoice, a prescription, a medical report, a fine — and the AI reads it and proposes concrete actions: adds an expense, creates an event, records a visit, sets a reminder or renames the file. You confirm, KidBox does the rest.

PDFs and images Automatic actions Expenses Medical visits Deadlines Opt-in
